About Us.

Arohi Verma

Meet Aarohi Verma — The Heart Behind the Sparkle Aarohi Verma is the visionary founder and creative soul behind our jewellery brand. With a passion for timeless elegance and a love for intricate craftsmanship, she curates each piece as a blend of tradition and modern charm. Aarohi believes that jewellery is not just an accessory — it's a story, a memory, a piece of art that speaks to the soul. Raised among the shimmer of heritage and inspired by the sparkle of today’s women, she set out on a mission to create pieces that empower, enchant, and elevate. Every collection reflects her belief: you don’t just wear jewellery, you wear confidence, history, and a little bit of magic.
